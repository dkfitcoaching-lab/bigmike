About Big Mike

Big Mike Ely is a 2x IFBB Pro Masters World Champion, 2x Olympian, and one of the most respected names in competitive bodybuilding. Coaching since 1996, Mike has built an operation that has produced hundreds of pro cards and transformed thousands of athletes — from first-time competitors to national-level champions across every division.

With a Bachelor’s in Nutritional Science and a Master’s in Exercise Science from Bowling Green State University, every protocol Mike writes is grounded in real science and pressure-tested through three decades of competition. No templates. No guesswork. Every athlete gets a strategy engineered for their body, their goals, and their timeline.
