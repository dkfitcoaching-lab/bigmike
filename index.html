The Inner Circle

This sport doesn't reward potential. It rewards preparation, precision, and decades of knowing what actually works.

Mike earned his IFBB Pro card in 2013 with legendary coach Hany Rambod in his corner. Two-time IFBB Pro Masters World Champion — back to back, 2024 and 2025. Two-time Mr. Olympia competitor. NPC Judge. Since 1996, he has coached hundreds of athletes to their own pro cards — not with theory, but with the knowledge that only comes from living it.

He and his wife Dejan run Center Stage Aesthetics out of Old School Iron Gym in Brook Park, Ohio — where the standard isn't negotiable.
