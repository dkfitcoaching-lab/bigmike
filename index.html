Three Decades on the Stage

This sport does not reward potential. It rewards preparation, precision, and decades of knowing what actually works.

Mike earned his IFBB Pro card in 2013 with legendary coach Hany Rambod in his corner. Two-time IFBB Pro Masters World Champion. Masters Mr. Olympia competitor. NPC Judge. Since 1996, he has coached over 30 athletes to their own pro cards — not with theory, but with the knowledge that only comes from living it.

Operating out of Old School Iron Gym in Ohio, Mike brings three decades of elite competition, deep pharmacology expertise, and stage strategy tested under the brightest lights in the sport. No templates. No shortcuts. Every program is built from scratch for the athlete standing in front of him.
