About

Mike Ely earned his IFBB Pro card in 2013 at the NPC Masters Nationals with Hany Rambod in his corner. He is a 2x IFBB Pro Masters World Champion, Olympian, and NPC Judge with over three decades in the sport.

He holds a BS in Nutritional Science and an MS in Exercise Science from Bowling Green State University. Since 1996, he has coached athletes across every division — bodybuilding, figure, bikini, physique, and wellness — with over 30 athletes earning their IFBB Pro status under his guidance.

His programs are built on real science, refined by decades of competition, and custom to every athlete.
