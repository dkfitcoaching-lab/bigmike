About

In high school, a guidance counselor told Mike Ely he’d never become a pro bodybuilder. In 2013, he won first place at nationals and earned his IFBB Pro card — with legendary coach Hany Rambod in his corner. Since then, Mike has become a 2x IFBB Pro Masters World Champion and 2x Olympian, and built one of the most respected coaching operations in the sport.

With a BS in Nutritional Science and an MS in Exercise Science from Bowling Green State University, Mike coaches everyone — from first-time gym-goers to national-level competitors across every division. Bodybuilding, figure, bikini, physique, wellness. His programs are built on real science, refined by decades of competition, and custom to every athlete.
